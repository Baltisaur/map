About me

I'm a 22 year old Ruby and Rails developer and university student living in Brisbane, Australia. I enjoy riding bicycles, motorbikes, taking photos and travelling.

My love of computers started when the old man showed me Prince of Persia on a 386. I bounced from a gamer interested in PC hardware, to a Linux fantatic who would install a distribution on anything with 1's and 0's before developing a genuine passion for software.

Since February 2008 I've been employed part-time as a Ruby on Rails developer for Everyday Hero, a service for empowering not for profits with the tools to multiply their fundraising efforts.

I'm studying a Bachelor of Information Technology full-time at Griffith University where I'm in my second year and loving it.

You can follow me on Twitter, hear my musical taste on Last.fm or browse my photos on Flickr. I'm also on Facebook, but I don't visit very often.

Feel free to drop me an e-mail, tate@tatey.com
