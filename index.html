About

Tate Johnson is a full-stack developer at LIFX who has a knack for seeing how everything fits together. Simplicity and defaults are my guiding principles. These days I live in Brisbane and commute regularly to Melbourne.

Some of my software includes:

  • Antenna Mate, takes the guess work out of pointing a TV antenna.
  • Tug, hosted continuous deployment from GitHub to Heroku
  • Maps for Antenna Mate, browse TV transmitters over Google Maps
  • Next Stop, alerts you when its time to hop off the bus

You can follow me on Twitter, fork my code on GitHub, spectate my shots on Dribbble, hear what I listen to on Last.fm, or subscribe to my blog's RSS feed.
